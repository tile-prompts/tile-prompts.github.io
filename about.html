About
Tile-Prompts is a static site for building randomized writing prompts by drawing tiles.
There are five tile types: Roles, Traits, Situations, a single Place, and a single Task.
Tips:
  • Left-click a tile to remove it.
  • Right-click a tile to reroll it.
  • Use the Sets tab to enable or disable content packs.
